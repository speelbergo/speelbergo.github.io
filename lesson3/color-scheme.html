Description

Mars is the red planet so it would be obscene not to include a shade of the rust iron red that it's known for. However, Mars today is springing forth with lively colonies, so a rich, forest green represents the budding flora. Light and dark browns tie the whole site together, representing rich, earthly, soil from which all life springs forth.
