Target Audience

The Target audience for this site would be Earthlings wanting the latest news and updates on the Martian colonies. These could be prospective visitors or immigrants to Mars, friends and family of the colonists, parties looking to fund or support the colonies, or just curiosity seekers.

Persona 1

Ed and Edna Jones struck it rich in the emerging cadmium trade and are looking to leave their cares behind by retiring to the Red Planet. The site will answer the following questions for them:

  • How's the climate control and structural integrity of the bio domes coming along?

  • What's the latest on diversity of food staples?

  • Is the arts community progressing?

  • How reliable are the communication networks between Earth and Mars?

  • Are there a solid number of retirees there with whom they could interact with?

Persona 2

Ingrid Schtickelgruber is the minister of finance for the united state of Hungary and Prussia. She is overseeing a study as to the potential benefits that might come from sending a delegation from her country to the Red Planet. She's interested in the following information:

    >
  • How is the genetic diversity among fellow colonists?

  • Is there a problem with racism or other discrimination towards new immigrants?

  • What's the crime and safety statistics?

  • Will there be jobs and shelter for new residents?

  • Are there adequate foodstuffs and supplies for a large group of settlers?
