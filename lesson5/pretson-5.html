Preston Colony founding

The world famous Preston, Idaho was once a small agricultural town completely unknown outside the region. That is until the landmark feature film Napoleon Dynamite was released. Although it made a noticeable yet short-lived impact upon its first release, it was revived a generation later and became a cultural touchstone that ushered in an era of peace the world had never heretofore seen. It's no wonder then that the first permanent post-terrestrial colony was named after this famous utopia, including a reproduction of the bronze statue of its most revered folk hero.
