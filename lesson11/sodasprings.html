Soda Licious Living

The members of the Soda Springs collective have something relevant to brag about: the only Martian spring. Not only is this the only miraculous source of liquid on the surface of our dear red planet, but it's carbonated! Our scientists are hypothesizing that it has to do with the carbon dioxide in the atmosphere outside the dome, but the members of our collective don't care: they're just here to enjoy it and the industry/trade it brings our fair dome.
